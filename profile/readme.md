## The Orchestra
 
Every Tuesday evening, nearly forty musicians from across the University of Edinburgh — and from all over the world — gather to rehearse in the Reid Concert Hall. Since 1983, the Edinburgh University Chamber Orchestra has brought ambitious, high-quality classical music to audiences across Scotland and beyond.
 
We perform two main concerts each season, collaborate annually with Edinburgh Studio Opera on a full-scale opera production, and host the Edinburgh University Concerto Competition. Our members also form smaller ensembles for community outreach, bringing live music to schools and care homes across the city.
 
We have toured Spain, Germany, the Netherlands, Italy, France, the Czech Republic, Austria, Belgium, Ireland, and the Scottish Highlands. We have performed as part of the Edinburgh Festival Fringe, and collaborated with orchestras including the Roma Tre Orchestra and Glasgow University's Kelvin Ensemble.

## The Website
 
This is the source code for our online home — two connected but independent sites that serve different purposes.
 
<br>
 
### The Main Site — [www.eu-co.co.uk](https://www.eu-co.co.uk)
 
The public face of EUCO. Here you'll find:
 
- **Concerts** — our upcoming season with dates, venues, programmes, and ticket links, alongside a full archive of past performances
- **About** — our story from 1983 to the present, the conductors and guest artists who have led us, our opera collaboration, the concerto competition, and audition information
- **Community** — our outreach programme bringing music beyond the concert hall
- **Support** — information for sponsors and partners, and how to donate
 
Concert data is managed through a simple Google Spreadsheet. When a committee member updates a concert date or adds a new event, the website reflects the change automatically — no coding required.
 
<br>
 
### The Education Hub — [education.eu-co.co.uk](https://education.eu-co.co.uk)
 
A growing collection of resources about the music we love:
 
- **Composer Database** — biographies and key works of over 60 composers whose music has appeared in our concerts, browsable by era from Baroque to the 20th century
- **Programme Notes** — essays written by our members and conductors exploring the works we perform, from Mendelssohn's Scottish Symphony to Beethoven's Second
- **Articles** — deeper explorations of specific pieces, with embedded recordings and performances
- **Learn** — an introduction to the chamber orchestra, its instruments, and what makes it unique
 
The education site has its own distinct visual identity — an editorial, journal-like design with warm amber tones — reflecting its role as a reading and learning space rather than a concert listings page.

## Get Involved
 
**As a musician** — Auditions take place every September at the start of the academic year. All orchestral instruments are welcome. Prepare a short solo piece of your choice (under five minutes) and be ready for a brief sight-reading excerpt. Sign-up details are posted on our [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/edunichamberorchestra/) before the start of term.
 
**As a listener** — Our concerts are held at the Reid Concert Hall and other venues across Edinburgh. Tickets are affordable and often free. Check the website for our upcoming season.
 
**As a supporter** — Whether you're a local business interested in sponsorship or an individual who believes in the power of student music, we'd love to hear from you. Visit our [support page](https://www.eu-co.co.uk/support-us) or [get in touch directly](https://www.eu-co.co.uk/contact-us).
